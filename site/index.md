# Ice in motion, resolved numerically. {: .reveal}

I'm a PhD researcher working on ice flow modeling — subglacial hydrology, sediment transport, and glacial erosion — built on GPU-accelerated PDE solvers. This site collects my projects, publications, and interactive visualizations.
{: .reveal}

Ice deforms as a shear-thinning fluid following Glen's flow law, $\dot{\varepsilon} = A\tau^n$, and much of my work amounts to solving that equation well, at scale, on real geometry.
{: .reveal}
